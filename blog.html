Blog

Hello! I’m Vivek Sharma, a passionate digital marketing professional based in Mohali, Punjab. Originally from Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh, I come from a close-knit family that has always been my source of inspiration. With an MCA degree and a growing interest in technology and creativity, I have immersed myself in the world of SEO, analytics, and digital branding.

My professional focus lies in SEO strategies, content creation, social media management, and analytics. I enjoy finding the balance between creativity and technical data-driven decisions to help brands grow their presence online. I am currently an intern at Shivah Web Tech, learning the core of digital marketing and gaining hands-on experience with real-world projects.

Life outside work is just as important to me. I love listening to music, playing volleyball, and exploring new tools. These hobbies keep me balanced and motivated, pushing me to be a better version of myself. Coming from a nuclear family, with a retired father from the Indian Army and a homemaker mother, I have learned the value of discipline, hard work, and patience. My siblings also inspire me with their dedication to their respective fields.

Through this blog, I want to share my journey — the ups and downs, lessons learned, and skills I am developing. My posts will talk about topics such as how SEO works, why personal branding matters, how small businesses can use social media effectively, and ways to stay motivated in a fast-paced digital world.

This website is more than just a portfolio. It is my way of documenting progress and encouraging others who are passionate about building a career in the tech and marketing industry. I believe continuous learning and collaboration are the keys to success, and I aim to connect with people who share the same passion.

Thank you for visiting my blog — whether you are a student, professional, or just curious about digital marketing, I hope you find my journey relatable and insightful. Let’s grow and learn together!
